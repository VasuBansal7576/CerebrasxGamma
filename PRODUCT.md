# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Consumers, fleet operators, home warranty reviewers, and consumer-protection teams who need to decide whether a repair, contractor, or service quote is safe to approve. They are usually under time pressure and do not want a chatbot; they want a sourced audit they can use in a negotiation.

## Product Purpose

QuoteSquad turns pasted or uploaded quotes into confidence-scored findings, source citations, explicit provider gaps, and ready-to-use negotiation artifacts. Success means the user can see which line items are worth challenging, which findings are weak, and what supporting proof is still missing.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, forensic. The product should feel like a trustworthy evidence desk: quiet enough for repeated use, but opinionated about uncertainty and source quality.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page gloss, decorative dashboards, fake marketplace recommendations, unsourced savings claims, and any UI that hides missing providers behind confident copy.

## Design Principles

- Evidence before persuasion: every claim shows its source, confidence, or gap.
- The first screen is the tool, not a marketing page.
- Uncertainty stays visible and useful.
- Dense information is acceptable when it improves a negotiation decision.
- Free/public data is labeled as such; paid or legal-provider gaps are not disguised.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-operable forms, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, semantic headings and landmarks, and no color-only status communication.
